About Me

A Long Story Short

Brief Summary:

I grew up in a small town north of Salt Lake City, Utah. I grew up playing lots of sports such as soccer, basketball, and tennis to name a few. I also enjoyed playing video games and watching movies with family and friends. After graduating high school, I applied to serve a religious mission and was assigned to Taipei, Taiwan. I spent approximately two years there, maade lots of friends, learned Chinese, and became a better, more responsible person. After my time there, I returned home and began attending Brigham Young University pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Economics.

Misc. Information

Outside of work and school you'll find me doing a variety of things. I enjoy reading, participating in service projects, and going on runs. I like to give myself a challenge and I'm currently training to run a marathon later this year!

I'm not much good at writing/designing websites, but I enjoy new challenges and doing things I've never done before. For example, I've recently started to get into video editing and making an unpublished podcast with some friends and family to record memorable experiences.
